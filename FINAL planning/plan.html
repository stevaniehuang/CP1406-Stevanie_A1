Goals

The purpose of this site is to attract younger audience to join cosplay by putting the information about CossiePlay on the site and increase the revenue of the business.

Success Evaluation

The success can be evaluated through the increasing number of customers, especially teenagers, that join the CossiePlay party. By getting more visitors, the website has fulfilled its purpose.
It can also be measured by the number of orders that are received. If there are more orders received by the business, the website has shown its result.

Target Audience

This site is intended for: Young audience, such as teenagers or young adults. People with age range of 15 -25 years old.

The design strength to attract young audience are:

  • Make the content easy to read and understand
  • The content is scannable, not too many words
  • Include pictures to show the example of cosplaying
  • Use attractive colours
  • Use appropriate font style and size
